Pizza

Ingredients

  • ground beef
  • mozzarella cheese
  • spaghetti sauce
  • sliced pepperoni
  • sliced mushrooms
  • sliced black olives

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C)
  2. Cook ground beef in a skillet over medium heat until no longer pink
  3. Fill a large pot with lightly salted water and bring to a rapid boil. Cook egg noodles at a boil until tender yet firm to the bite, about 5 minutes; drain.
  4. Combine beef, noodles, 1/2 mozzarella cheese, spaghetti sauce, pepperoni, mushrooms, and black olives in a large bowl
